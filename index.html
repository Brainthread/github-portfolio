My Work

AR Ship game


This game tests the player's multitasking skills like nothing before. Playing as the captain of a ship, the player has to press their arrow keys to steer the ship away from oncoming obstacles. At the same time, they have to direct their crew in order to solve on-board crises, using physical crewmate-tokens on a paper board to do so. The game was made in 2 weeks for the course DT2140 @ KTH.

Technologies used:

  • Unity3D
  • Vuforia AR
  • OBS virtual camera
