Trackquiz


A react application where users log in via their google account, and host music quizes by providing Spotify playlist links. The app looks through the list and generates trivia questions based on the tracks within. Other users can then join the quiz room and participate in the quiz through their own devices, in a realtime multiplayer fashion similar to applications like Kahoot or Mentimeter.

AR Ship game


This game tests the player's multitasking skills like nothing before. Playing as the captain of a ship, the player has to press their arrow keys to steer the ship away from oncoming obstacles. At the same time, they have to direct their crew in order to solve on-board crises, using physical crewmate-tokens on a paper board to do so.

The system uses Unity as an engine, OBS to read data from a webcamera, and Vuforia to track the tokens caught in those images.

The game was made in 2 weeks for the course DT2140 @ KTH.

My contributions:

  • Conceptualization
  • Project Management
  • Game Logic
  • User Testing

Who Am I?


Hello and welcome to my hub of projects!
